# Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 13, 2026

OneTaskFocus is designed to be simple and privacy-friendly.

## Data Collection

OneTaskFocus does not require account creation.

OneTaskFocus does not collect personal data through a backend service.

The app stores focus session data locally on the user’s device so the user can review session history and streaks.

## Notifications

If the user enables notifications, OneTaskFocus may send local notifications on the device when a focus session ends.

These notifications are generated locally and are not sent through a remote server controlled by the developer.

## Analytics

OneTaskFocus does not include third-party analytics in the current version of the app.

## Third-Party Services

OneTaskFocus does not require sign-in and does not use a cloud account to provide its core functionality.

## Data Sharing

OneTaskFocus does not sell or share personal information with third parties through a backend service in the current version of the app.

## Contact

If you have privacy questions, use the contact details listed in `SUPPORT.md`.
